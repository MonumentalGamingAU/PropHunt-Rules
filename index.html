Monumental Gaming - Prop Hunt

Welcome!

Welcome to Monumental Gaming Prop Hunt. Prop Hunt plays much like a game of Hide and Seek. Players on the 'Prop Team' turn themselves into various objects throughout the map and are given a 30 second set up time to hide. Afterwards players on the 'Hunter Team' must find and kill them in the given time period. At the end of each round, teams are swapped. Respawns aren't permitted until the end of each round. If you enjoyed your stay, visit our forums at monumentalgaming.net

1. Server Rules

  • Do not advertise other servers.
  • Do not ask for a rank up; be it respected, mod, admin or VIP.
  • Do not ask other players for points.
  • Do not harass or bully other players.

  • Do not use cheats, hax, scripts or any other type of game modification. Doing so will result in a permanent ban from the Monumental Community.
  • Excessive prop killing is not allowed.
  • Hunters must be able to both see and shoot a prop without the use of explosives.

  • Points spent in the Points Shop can not be reimbursed by staff.
  • Prolonged periods of inactivity will result in being kicked.
  • Threatening (or undertaking) to DDOS the server or any of our players will result in a permanent ban from the Monumental Community.
  • When casting a vote kick, discuss why in chat first and give time for the person being kicked to respond e.g. Votekick on Jester for hiding in unreachable spots, any objections?
    • Do not start a vote if reasonable objections are raised.
    • Do not start a vote if staff are online.
  • When using any donator commands (!gag, !mute) you must inform staff via admin chat (@) before doing so.
  • You must not switch teams to play as either prop or hunter in consecutive rounds.
