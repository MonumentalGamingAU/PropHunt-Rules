Monumental Gaming - Prop Hunt

Welcome!

Welcome to Monumental Gaming Prop Hunt. Prop Hunt plays much like a game of Hide and Seek. Players on the 'Prop Team' turn themselves into various objects throughout the map and are given a 30 second set up time to hide. Afterwards players on the 'Hunter Team' must find and kill them in the given time period. At the end of each round, teams are swapped. If you enjoyed your stay, visit our forums at monumentalgaming.net

1. Server Rules

  • Do not abuse commands (e.g. !unstuck) or use exploits to gain an unfair advantage.
  • Do not exploit the map or leave map boundaries.
  • Do not advertise other servers.
  • Do not ask other players for points.
  • Do not harass or bully other players for any reason.
    • Doxing (revealing someone's real-life information) will result in a permanent ban.
  • Do not exacerbate negative situations.
  • Do not metagame. This means giving away otherwise unknown information through chat, Steam, third-party programs etc.
  • Do not prop surf.
  • Spam of any form is not allowed (e.g. microphone, text chat).
  • The use of cheats or third-party programs to give yourself an unfair advantage will result in a permanent ban.
  • Prop killing is not allowed.
  • Hunters must be able to both see and shoot a prop without the use of explosives.
  • Large bodies of water are considered out of bounds.
  • Players must not attempt to impersonate Prop Hunt staff and/or players in anyway (unless you have permission).
  • Prolonged periods of inactivity will result in being kicked.
  • DDoS threats and/or DDoSing will result in a permanent ban.
  • Do not use any donator or respected commands while staff are on the server.
  • You must not switch teams to play as either prop or hunter in consecutive rounds.

2. Useful Information

  • C to prop lock.
  • If no staff are online and an individual is breaking the rules, record or screenshot their behaviour and post a ban request on our forums.
  • If you are unsure about a spot being legal, just ask.
  • Left click to become a prop.
  • Use @ before a message to contact online staff.
  • Use F3 to taunt.
  • Use F4 to open the shop, where you can purchase hats, trails and more with in-game points. Some shop items require specific ranks (e.g. donator).
